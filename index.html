Structure your handover using ISBAR

I

Identify

Introduce yourself and the patient.

S

Situation

Explain the immediate problem.

B

Background

Give only the relevant clinical background.

A

Assessment

State what you think is happening.

R

Recommendation

Say what you need and how urgently.
